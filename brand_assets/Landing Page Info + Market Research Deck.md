## What This Page Is For

A landing page for a free Discord community targeting beginner copywriters. The goal is one action: get them to click the join button and enter the Discord. No purchase required, no email opt-in - just join.

---

## The Offer (What They Get For Free)

**1. 30-Day Beginner Roadmap**
A structured day-by-day guide showing exactly what to do to go from zero to landing first clients. Removes the "I don't know what to do today" problem entirely.

**2. Monthly Free Coaching Call**
One live group coaching call per month with KJ. Open Q&A, copy reviews, strategy. Monthly cadence keeps it high value.

**3. Copy & Outreach Reviews by Experts**
Submit your copy or outreach messages and get feedback from experienced writers inside the community. Know if you're improving instead of guessing.

**4. Monthly Challenges With Cash Prizes**
Gamified monthly competitions with real cash prizes. Three tracked categories:
- Most outreach sent
- Most positive replies received
- Best accountability streak

Winners verified by KJ on a call before prizes are paid out. One person can only win one category per month.

**5. Like-Minded Community**
A community of people at the same stage doing the same work. Accountability partners, engagement, peer support. Active chat, not a dead server.
